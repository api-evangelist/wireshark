Sharkfest ’10 ended a week ago today and I’m still reeling. The conference started with a keynote from Van Jacobson and ended with one from Harry Saal , two monumental figures in our industry and very nice people to boot. Attendees traveled from all over the globe, from large companies to single-person operations.
