The big news in the tech industry this week is The Heartbleed Bug , a vulnerability that affects a large portion of secure web sites on the Internet. I updated the Wireshark and WinPcap web sites on Monday (along with reissuing and revoking certificates) shortly after OS patches were released. Our web sites are protected going forward, but what about the past?
